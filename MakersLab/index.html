Create something wonderful together.

Come join our local community of craftsmanship,
both traditional trades and modern techniques.

Work with the technologies that have
thrown doors open for creative potential.

Learn from each other about the arts of
fine handmade woodwork and more.

We are an organization with one goal in mind:
uniting the makers, fixers, tinkerers, nerds,
and curious minds of our communities.
Sharing tools is only the start of what we're about!

Perks of joining:
  • Sharing tools means no need to store them yourself.
  • Learn new skills from local artisans.
  • Work in a space with like-minded individuals, not in the garage.

A project by Clancy Clawson.
